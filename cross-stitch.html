Cross Stitch

Cross stich is a form of hand sewing and counted-thread embroidery. As the name suggests, the stitches are made in an X-shape (or cross) and are done on an evenweave fabric, such as linen or aida cloth. Cross stitching has become very popular in recent years and, as a result, there are now kits available at most craft stores with pre-cut fabric and embroidery floss to help you get started on your cross stitching journey!
